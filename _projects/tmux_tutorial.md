An interactive, hands-on tutorial for learning **tmux** — the terminal multiplexer that every developer should know but few take the time to learn properly.

Whether you're SSHing into servers, running multiple processes, or just tired of opening 15 terminal tabs — tmux is the answer, and this tutorial gets you there fast.
